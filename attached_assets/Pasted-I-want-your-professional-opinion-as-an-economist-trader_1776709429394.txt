3. **Sell Velocity** - How fast similar items get sold (from transaction history)

4. **Scarcity** - Number of unique sellers (fewer sellers = less competition)

5. **Freshness** - How recently the item was listed
   - This should have HIGHER weight than I initially thought
   - Items listed in last 5-10 minutes are prime sniping opportunities
   - Items older than 2-3 hours lose value significantly
   - Reason: New listings haven't been seen by many buyers yet

- A fresh listing (5-10 minutes old) is much more valuable than an older one
